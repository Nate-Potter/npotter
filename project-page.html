Build a Home NAS with Raspberry Pi

Cut the cost of an expensive NAS device and build your own using a Raspberry Pi! This especially works great if you have an extra hard drive laying around.

What is a NAS?
A NAS is a network attached storage device, which is basically a storage drive that you can access from your WiFi (think home cloud). This allows you to easily work on the same files while jumping between devices on your network. You can also upload movies to the storage, allowing you to "stream" the movies on your devices. Another option is to connect your home security cameras to the NAS, which will let you view and store footage on your network.
