This is the first blog post on this site

This week I finally started my first course on Udemy. It is Jose Portilla's "The Complete SQL Bootcamp 2022: Go from Zero to Hero."
In this course Jose teaches PostgreSQL using PgAdmin as the development platform. I do not have any previous experience with SQL, but
I am very excited to dive into this course. The course consists of 12 sections and has about 9 hours of content. So far I have just
completed the first section, which involves an introduction, course overview, and the installation of PostgreSQL and PgAdmin. Stay
tuned for updates of my progress!
